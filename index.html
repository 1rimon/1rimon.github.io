01 — About

Who Am I?

I am Md. Nur Islam Rimon — a B.Sc. graduate in Electrical & Electronic Engineering (GPA: 3.72) from American International University-Bangladesh (AIUB). I currently serve as Engineer (Electrical) in the Maintenance Department at Lantabur Apparels Ltd., a sister concern of Lantabur Group, since January 2022.

I have played a pivotal role in the planning, erection, installation, and commissioning of major industrial projects — including Lantabur Apparels Ltd. (Fabric Dyeing & Finishing Unit) and Yamin Accessories Ltd. (Yarn Dyeing & Packaging Unit). I specialize in 11 kV/0.415kV Substations, HT/LT Switchgear, Transformers, and Distribution Panels.

I have led technical audit preparations for RSC, NIRAPON, and buyer technical audits, ensuring strict adherence to BNBC, NFPA, and IEEE standards. I am skilled in designing and modifying Single Line Diagrams (SLD) to optimize factory power distribution and load management.
